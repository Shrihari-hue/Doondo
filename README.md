# Doondo
A city-wise part-time and full-time job marketplace platform that connects local businesses (shops, hotels, hospitals, delivery companies, offices, and small companies) with job seekers looking for flexible work opportunities.

This platform enables businesses to subscribe and post job vacancies, while job seekers can discover and apply for jobs near their location.

⸻

Overview

The Job Marketplace App is designed to solve a major problem in local employment:
finding short-term, flexible, and nearby jobs quickly.

Businesses often struggle to find workers quickly, while students and job seekers struggle to discover nearby opportunities.
This platform bridges that gap using location-based job discovery.

⸻

Key Features

For Job Seekers
	•	User registration and login
	•	Create a personal profile
	•	Add skills and job preferences
	•	Search jobs by city or nearby location
	•	Filter jobs by:
	•	Part-time / Full-time
	•	Salary range
	•	Industry
	•	Distance
	•	View job details
	•	Apply for jobs
	•	Track application status
	•	Save/bookmark jobs

⸻

For Employers / Businesses
	•	Business registration
	•	Create employer profile
	•	Post job vacancies
	•	Manage job listings
	•	View applicants
	•	Communicate with job seekers
	•	Manage subscription plan

⸻

Location-Based Job Search

The platform supports area-wise job discovery.

Users can:
	•	Find jobs near their location
	•	Filter jobs within:
	•	1 km
	•	5 km
	•	10 km

This makes the experience similar to ride-hailing or delivery apps where nearby opportunities appear instantly.
